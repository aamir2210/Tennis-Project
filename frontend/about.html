About Us

Court Crafters was established in 2017. We are a small team of passionate tennis players that noticed a gap in the online marketplace for dedicated tennis stores with a variety of tennis products! The goal of our website is to provide you, the user, with a fantastic browsing experience for all things tennis. You can browse all of our tennis related products on the products tab. Or you can track the amount of calories you burned during your recent tennis match. You can even watch tennis videos to improve your skill. We are headquartered in Toronto Ontario, Canada.
